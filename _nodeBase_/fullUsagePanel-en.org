** ~BISDF Key Concepts And Terminology~ (ByStar Internet Services Development Framework)
** Typed Function   --- Programming Language Construct (typed args and typed return values)
** Operation  --- (Arguments, Result, Error) A function that returns An OpOutcome and accepts an opOutcome as an argument and whose arguments are typed.
** Operation opOutcome  --- Results, Status, Errors, Failures, Reports
** Expectation-Complete-Operation --- An Operation capable of reporting all of its inputs and of its OpOutcome.
Expectation-Complete-Operation can be invoked in such a way that they do not invoke the operation but that instead
they report their expected parameters and their expected Outcome.
** Command-Service --- An Expectation-Complete-Operation Which can be invoked as a command, a service or an operation.
** A Command, is invocation of a Command-Service from the command line.
** A Service Performer, is invocation of a Command-Service through Remote-Operations machinary
** A Service Invoker, is invocation of a Command-Service that produces inputs for a Remote-Operations machinary and which then receives the Outcome.
** An Expectation-Complete-Operation-User-Interface --- A User-Interface that through the use of Expectation-Complete-Operation
can produce a generic but complete interface for invocation as a command or a service invoker.
** Wrapped-Operations --- Operations invoked during the invokation of an Expectation-Complete-Operation.
